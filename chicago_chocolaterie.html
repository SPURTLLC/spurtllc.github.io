The Problem

Bringing Brussels To Chicago Illinois

“Chicago Chocolaterie (CHI CHOCOLATE) carries a renowned recipe passed down through generations in Brussels—now reimagined in the heart of the Windy City, Chicago, Illinois.”


Problems to Solve
Details

The problems at hand are:
1. A brand that infuses well into the Chicago culture and keeps its Brussels roots.
2. A brand that is social media friendly.
3. A logo that works well on windows.

The Solution

Visual Outcome


1. Chicago Chocolatarie's logo is supported by reimageined image of the Brussels flag, where it is placed strategicaly at the bottom of the typography to symbolise its roots and how the Brussels recipe supports Chicago Chocolatarie.


2. Curation of a circular icon and illustrative pattern to scale into a social media profile pictures as well as banner.
3. Pairing a thin typography set to contrast with the bold illustrative design allows the logo to be noticibal but at the same time tolerable on glass windows.
